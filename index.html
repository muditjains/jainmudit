A little about me

Hi there! I'm a Software Engineer and Computer Science student with a passion for exploring the great outdoors and capturing beautiful moments through photography. In my free time, you'll often find me hiking in the mountains or planning my next travel adventure.

There's nothing quite like the feeling of standing at the top of a mountain and taking in the breathtaking views, or discovering new cultures and cuisines in far-off places. When I'm not exploring the world, I'm usually working on my coding skills and keeping up with the latest developments in the tech industry. As a software engineer, I love solving complex problems and building innovative solutions that make a real difference in people's lives. But no matter what I'm doing, I always try to stay curious, open-minded, and optimistic about what the future holds. Life is an adventure, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.

University of Central Florida
